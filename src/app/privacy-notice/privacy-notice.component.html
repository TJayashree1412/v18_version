IBM Confidential! Statement of usage policy


The US HCAM tool is a web based application and it is used to manage resources in the US Host Country Assignment Model by automating the compensation and on-boarding / off-boarding processes for the resources.

This tool contains details of Employee PI/SPI data such as Name, Email ID, Band, Date of Birth, SSN, Bank A/C number, Compensation, PMR, Incentive / Bonus for US HCAM Assignment.

PI/SPI data is collected from Professional Market Place (PMP), Operational Data Manager (ODM) and some of the details are filled/calculated in the tool.

Employee PI/SPI data which is collected and stored in US HCAM Database will be kept strictly confidential and will be obtained and used only in accordance with applicable Privacy legislation.

Employee information will only be made available within IBM to other individual on a "need to know basis".

As a user of HCAM tool, I will adhere to the Business Conduct Guidelines and Data Protection Guidance.

Please refer to the IBM Privacy statement for an understanding of IBM's General Privacy Practices.

I will ensure information in the tool is not duplicated, distributed, shared or removed from IBM premises or sent through the IBM network without prior management approval.
